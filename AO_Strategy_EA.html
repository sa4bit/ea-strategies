AO Strategy EA

üìò AO Strategy Description

This EA uses the Awesome Oscillator (AO) across 3 selectable timeframes (HTF, ITF, ETF) to confirm momentum direction. Each timeframe can act as a filter independently ‚Äî only when all selected timeframes agree, a trade signal is triggered.

The AO bar color is the key: green bars indicate bullish momentum, and red bars indicate bearish momentum. For example, if AO is green on H4 and H1, and also turns green on M15 (Entry Timeframe), a Buy trade is triggered. The Exit Timeframe determines which TF‚Äôs AO reversal triggers the trade closure.

Entry Timeframe (Dropdown): HTF / ITF / ETF

Exit Timeframe (Dropdown): HTF / ITF / ETF

‚úÖ Entry Conditions:

  • Buy: AO bar is green on Entry Timeframe and also on HTF/ITF if enabled
  • Sell: AO bar is red on Entry Timeframe and also on HTF/ITF if enabled
  • Exit: Based on the selected Exit Timeframe when AO bar color flips

‚úÖ Optional Filters:

  • ADX Filter: If enabled, confirms strength via ADX > 25 and +DI > -DI for Buy, -DI > +DI for Sell
  • MA Filter (EMA/SMA 200): Confirms price is above/below MA for directional bias
  • Both filters can be enabled separately or together and work on the selected Entry Timeframe

üîÅ Manual Trade Handling:

If you manually close a trade, the EA will not re-enter immediately. It will wait for a new qualifying signal to appear, preventing noisy re-entries.
